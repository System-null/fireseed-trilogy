# Code of Conduct

We follow the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct.html) v2.1.

## Expectations
- Be respectful, inclusive, and considerate.
- Welcome feedback and collaborate in good faith.
- Prioritize community safety and long-term stewardship.

## Unacceptable behavior
Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, or publication of private information are not tolerated.

## Reporting
Report incidents to the maintainers via private channels listed in the repository profile. Reports are handled promptly and confidentially.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including warnings or removal from project spaces, guided by the Contributor Covenant enforcement ladder.
